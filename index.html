Research Publications & Scientific Contributions

Prevalence of High-Risk HPV in HIV-Infected Women in Nigeria

This study examines the prevalence of high-risk HPV genotypes among HIV-infected women in Nigeria. Findings reveal a significant presence high-risk strains; HPV16 and HPV18, emphasizing the need for expanded testing and preventive measures to reduce cervical cancer cases.

Comparing Cobas 6800/8800 and CAP/CTM for HIV Viral Load Testing

This study compared Cobas 6800/8800 with CAP/CTM for HIV viral load testing in Nigeria. Analysis of 100 samples showed high correlation and 94.2% agreement, confirming Cobas 6800/8800 as reliable for high-volume labs.

Evaluating Self-Sampling for COVID-19 Testing in Nigeria

The study tested a self-sampling model for COVID-19 to reduce contact at testing centers. Among 216 participants, results matched in 204 cases, while six showed discrepancies, with self-collected samples testing positive but professionally collected ones negative.
